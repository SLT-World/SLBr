[Dialogs]
- Window Title: Error, Warning, Information, Success, Alert
- Headline: Capital letters for every first char
- Description: 1-2 sentences
- Actions: "OK"
- Examples:
Error, Connection Failed, The server could not be reached. Check your internet connection and try again.
Warning, Unsaved Changes, Closing now will discard any unsaved work. Do you want to continue?
Information, New Version Available, A newer version is available for download.
Success, Upload Complete, Files uploaded successfully.
